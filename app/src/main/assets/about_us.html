Welcome To Arihant Education Group

Since its inception, by Shri Jayant Kasliwal under Arihant Education and Hospital Society, in 2006 the institute has marked an epoch in innovative education. The institute has doggedly pursued new and sometimes implausible objectives and has been insatiable in providing quality education and eventually it has been accredited with ‘B’ grade by NAAC which stands to be the acme of academic achievemen.. At present the institute offers instructions in a host of courses including B.Ed, B.com, BBA, BSW, Arts,MBA, and BBA & MBA in Hospital Administration. The college is approved by University Grants Commission (UGC), National Council for Teacher Education (NCTE), (WRC) Bhopal, AICTE and affiliated to Devi Ahilya Vishwavidyalaya, Indore.

Student discipline, Faculty governance, academic ambience, value added programs, focused curriculum for all round development and ever modernizing infrastructure support are the hallmark of the organization.

The campus is located within 1 k.m. from the DAVV Takshashila Campus on the main Khandwa Road, which is the main Educational belt of well-established schools and colleges in Indore. The sprawling premises of Arihant Education Group is constructed as per the norms of UGC and NCTE and has all necessary amenities needed for helping the students to develop a holistic and integrated personality, besides seeking education. The college is spread in a 5.5 acres lush green campus with the buildings comprising all modern amenities. The college has utilized its human and infrastructure resources in the best manner to get excellent results.

Education at Arihant is not only about books, but working on over all grooming of our students. So besides the best academic practices, many co curricular activities like personality development classes, seminars, conferences and various talk shows were organized by the Institute.

Expert speakers from various fields were invited for delivering their lectures so that our students get a 360° exploration . Committed to imparting quality & innovative education to our students, we believe in providing both roots of value & wings of Inspiration to our students. And what will be better than celebrating various festivals to enjoy our diversity & shaping our values. College celebrates various festivals like Makarsankranti, Holi, Navratri, Ganesh Utsav and many more for creating harmony & sense of brotherhood among the students. The Institute is committed to provide confident, dedicated value laden & well trained skillful teachers to create foundation for our students.

Moving towards the academic & College life of our students at Arihant, Besides the college level awareness Programs like awareness for breast cancer, AIDS, females infanticides/ self-defense /Environmental protection, plantation drives, various competitions both at intra & inter college were conducted in almost all the cultural fields be it Singing “Swaranjali” Dancing “Nritanjali”, Poetry “Kavyanjali”, etc along with Rangoli, Mehndi , Face, Painting, Poster Making, Slogan, Essay Writing and many more.

Students of Arihant also participated in various social service drives organized at College & University level like NSS activities which also included activities like stationary distribution to the students belonging to BPL class, self defence classes, yoga classes, Adult education drives and the most successful campaign by our Prime Minister Narendra Modi Ji ,i.e. Swatch Bharat Abhiyan and Digital India.
