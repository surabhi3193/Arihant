Welcome To Arihant Education Group

Since its inception, by Shri Jayant Kasliwal under Arihant Education and Hospital Society, in 2006 the institute has marked an epoch in innovative education. The institute has doggedly pursued new and sometimes implausible objectives and has been insatiable in providing quality education and eventually it has been accredited with ‘B’ grade by NAAC which stands to be the acme of academic achievemen.. At present the institute offers instructions in a host of courses including B.Ed, B.com, BBA, BSW, Arts,MBA, and BBA & MBA in Hospital Administration. The college is approved by University Grants Commission (UGC), National Council for Teacher Education (NCTE), (WRC) Bhopal, AICTE and affiliated to Devi Ahilya Vishwavidyalaya, Indore.
